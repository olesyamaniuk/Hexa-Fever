Sort, Match & Solve – Enjoy the Ultimate Hexa Puzzle Challenge!

Game Description

Hexa Fever: Color Sort Puzzle is a fun and addictive logic game that will challenge your strategic thinking! Arrange the hexagonal tiles by color and create perfect matches to clear the board. With relaxing yet engaging gameplay, this puzzle game offers hundreds of levels, each more challenging than the last.

Test your problem-solving skills, enjoy smooth mechanics, and experience a satisfying color-sorting adventure. Whether you're looking for a casual brain teaser or a tough mental challenge, Hexa Fever has something for everyone!

    Challenging Gameplay – Test your logic by sorting colors into hexagonal grids.

  • 2

  • 3

  • 4

  • 5

  • 6

    Offline Mode – Play anywhere, anytime without an internet connection.

How to Play

  • Tap on a hexagonal cell to select a color.

  • Move the color to the correct hexagonal slot.

  • Match all colors in the grid to complete the level.

  • Use hints when stuck.

  • Progress through levels and challenge yourself with increasing difficulty.
